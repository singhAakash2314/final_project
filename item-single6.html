Glow of Hope –Painting by S L Haldankar

Glow of Hope, alternately titled "Woman With the Lamp", is a painting by S.L. Haldankar. The enclave is normally darkened, which highlights the subtlety of the glowing brass lamp in the piece. When the light is turned on, the painting reveals remarkably subtle shades of pink and lavender in the woman's sari. Opening the enclave's curtain leads to yet another distinct view of the painting, the natural light exposing even more subtle gradations and details.
